Project: Fruit Ninja Game

I coded a replica of fruit ninja using Python. I created groups for multiple fruits and a bomb and put them in a list. I iterate through that list dropping each fruit and a bomb at random spots at different speeds. If you click on a fruit you get a point, if you click on a bomb, you lose 2 points. If you reach 20 points you win. I made it so that if you click the space bar you can restart after losing or winning.

Project: Wedding Usher Planner

I coded a Wedding Usher planner using Python. I created multiple if statements checking first if you’re a friend or a family. After passing either, I use elif statements checking if you're a friend of the bride, friend of the groom, or friend of the bride and groom. If you’re family you sit in the front no matter who you're friends with to. If you're a friend of the bride, you sit on the left, a friend of the groom sits on the right, and a friend of both the bride and the groom sits in the front.
